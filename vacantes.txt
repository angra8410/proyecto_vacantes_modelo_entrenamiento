About the job
Are you looking to work for an innovative and fast-growing global technology company, 100% remotely?



We are looking for a highly driven and strategic Business Development Manager to join a company recognized for its database solutions and innovation in data replication technologies.
The ideal candidate will be responsible for identifying, recruiting, and enabling new partners focused on selling Standby for Microsoft SQL Server, building strong relationships, and driving growth through strategic partnerships in the SQL Server ecosystem.


The salary range is $1,800 USD to $2,800 USD per month, depending on the candidate’s experience and seniority.


Candidates from other Latin American countries may be considered. Candidates in a time zone outside of the Western Hemisphere will NOT be considered.

ALL RESUMES MUST BE SUBMITTED IN ENGLISH

Identify, recruit, and onboard new partners focused on selling Standby for MS SQL Server.

Establish and manage partner-specific revenue targets and performance metrics.
Collaborate with regional sales teams to align goals and market approaches.
Represent the company at industry events and partner conferences related to the SQL Server community.
Provide market intelligence and feedback to product teams to enhance our SQL Server offerings.
Design and deliver partner training programs and certification processes.

Proven experience in business development or partner management within the technology sector.
Strong understanding of the Microsoft SQL Server marketplace and ecosystem.
Demonstrated ability to build and nurture profitable partner relationships.
Excellent communication, negotiation, and presentation skills.
Experience working with technical solutions and the ability to clearly articulate their value.
Track record of meeting or exceeding revenue targets through partner channels.
Ability to work independently while collaborating effectively with regional sales teams.
Fluent in English — you must be able to have a fluent professional conversation in English.
