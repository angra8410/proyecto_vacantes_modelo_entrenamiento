About the job
Company Description

 
Kaeus is a consultancy firm that specializes in utilizing data to optimize business performance and enhance decision-making for clients. We offer services in data integration, modeling, governance, and solutions development, tailored to meet unique business goals. Our expert team is dedicated to delivering innovative and effective data-driven solutions while staying at the cutting edge of data analytics trends. With our personalized approach, we help clients remain competitive and achieve success through reliable, high-quality data solutions.


 Role Description

 
This is a remote, contract position for a Power BI Developer. The role involves working closely with stakeholders to design, develop, and deploy Power BI dashboards and visual reports. Responsibilities include performing data modeling, integrating data sources, and optimizing data flow. The Power BI Developer will also collaborate with teams to maintain data governance and meet analytics-related business requirements.


 Qualifications

 
Proficiency in Data Modeling and creating interactive dashboards with Power BI
Strong Analytical Skills for understanding and interpreting large datasets
Experience with Data Warehousing and "Extract, Transform, Load (ETL)" processes
Ability to work effectively in a remote collaborative environment
Detail-oriented with strong problem-solving and organizational skills
Bachelor’s degree in a relevant field, such as Computer Science, Data Analytics, or related discipline
Understanding of business intelligence tools and practices is a plus
